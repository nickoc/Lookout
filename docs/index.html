Why It Matters

What Local-First Makes Possible

Four capabilities that are structurally impossible in any centralized model.

Honest Scoring

No sponsor bias. No monetization pressure to rank paying brands higher. The algorithm serves only you, and you can inspect it yourself.

Adversarial Due Diligence

Pull litigation, complaints, and FDD red flags without any platform filtering. No structural conflict — when franchisors don't pay, the system is truly neutral.

Cross-Platform Intelligence

Not limited to one platform's catalog. Scout agents pull from Franchise Direct, FranNet, Franchise Gator, IFA, state registrations, and more.

Reversed Information Asymmetry

You have deep due diligence on the franchisor. They know nothing about you until you choose to share. That's a profound power shift for a $50K-$500K decision.
